Calvin's Favorite Movies

My top 5 movies of all time

1. American Fiction

A black novelist who is tired of the establisment profiting from the exploitation of Black sterotypes creates a pen name to write novels that propel him into the hypocrisy he claims to hate

2. All Dogs go to Heaven

A canine angel, Charlie, sneaks back to earth from heaven but ends up befriending an orphan girl who can speak to animals. In the process, Charlie learns that friendship is the most heavenly gift of all.

3. Monkey Man

An anonymous young man unleashes a campaign of vengeance against the corrupt leaders who murdered his mother and continue to systematically victimize the poor and powerless.

4. Payback

After a successful heist, Porter is left for dead. Once he recovers, he seeks vengeance and wants his share of the money.

5. Joker

Arthur Fleck, a party clown and a failed stand-up comedian, leads an impoverished life with his ailing mother. However, when society shuns him and brands him as a freak, he decides to embrace the life of chaos in Gotham City.
